About

How much do you know about sexual assault?

  • Most people never report their sexual assaults.

    The process of reporting can be traumatizing, exhausting, and impossible for many survivors.

  • After going through this terrible process, most victims have no way to track their rape kits.

    These rape kits are key pieces of DNA evidence required for most convictions. Some victims have waited 25 years, and others have never received their results. Most victims that experience this lose hope and choose to move on. By the time their kits are tested, they have no energy to relive the horrific details of their assault.

  • In the meantime, the perpetrators walk free.

    By creating a tracking system for rape kits, we hope to bring accountability to the system and bring peace of mind for victims.

  • Our tracking system will show victims the status of their kit at all times.

    This way, victims can continue to feel empowered throughout the reporting process.
